A brief history about Irfan Khan:

  • Sahabzade Irfan Ali Khan on 7 January 1967.
  • However, his scenes were reduced in the final cut.He played Lenin in a teleplay on Doordarshan titled Laal Ghaas Par Neele Ghode, based on a translation by Uday Prakash of a Russian play by Mikhail Shatrov.
  • On 23 February 1995, He married writer and fellow NSD graduate Sutapa Sikdar. They have two sons Babil and Ayan.
  • In 2015, he played the lead role in the film Piku alongside Deepika Padukone and Amitabh Bachchan. Khan co-starred in the 2015 film Jurassic World.
  • He played the adult version of Piscine "Pi" Molitor Patel in Ang Lee's film adaptation of Life of Pi, which became a critical and commercial success worldwide.
  • In 2009, he featured in the film Acid Factory.He appeared as an FBI agent in New York (2009),and opposite Natalie Portman as a Gujarati diamond merchant in the Mira Nair-directed segment of the twelve-part feature New York, I Love You..
  • He played a police inspector in the 2008 film Slumdog Millionaire, for which he and the cast of the movie won Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture. .
  • In March 2018, Khan revealed via Twitter that he had been diagnosed with a neuroendocrine tumor.He sought treatment in the UK for a year and returned to India in February 2019.
